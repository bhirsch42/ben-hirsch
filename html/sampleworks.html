A Bastion of Play
For an English project, I was required to write a paper about how a game of my choosing encourages "meaningful play." To accomplish this, I chose the game Bastion and created a program to drop in the words of the paper around the player's cursor while delivering vocal cues. Both of these are iconic elements to Bastion's gameplay.
